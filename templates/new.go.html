Morse Code (CW) and Digital Station

This station is located at the North-East corner of the parking lot. The station is working using Morse Code mode (a.k.a. CW) and Digital modes. You can listen to the exchange, but please don't disturb the operators. CW/Digital Station captain is Steve KO4VW. Please contact them if you have any questions.

Phone Station (SSB)

This station is located in Pavilion #2 outside the South-West corner of the soccer field. It mostly uses Phone mode (a.k.a. SSB or single-side band) but may occasionally use Digital modes. The station captains are Mark WA1NIP and Dave KO4USA.

Satellite Station

This station is located at the West side of the middle of the parking lot. It uses Amateur Radio satellites for contacts. You can watch the map and see when the next satellite will pass our location. Station captains are Daryl K4RGK and Scott KB4KBS. Please contact them if you have any questions.

Free VHF Station

This station is located at the tent next to the CW station. It uses the VHF bands, including "the magic band" i.e., the 6-meter band. It also uses other bands above 50MHz. This station operates at frequencies not in use by the three main transmitters and satellite stations. The station captain is Robert AJ4RJ.

GOTA (Get On The Air) Station

The GOTA station is located behind the Main picnic pavilion, on the East side of the upper soccer field. GOTA stations may operate on any Field Day band, HF or VHF, and are limited to one GOTA station transmitted signal at any time. The GOTA station may be operated by any person licensed since the previous yearâ€™s Field Day, regardless of license class. It may also be operated by a generally inactive licensee. Non-licensed persons may participate under the direct supervision of an appropriate control operator. Timo K4TMO is the station captain, assisted by co-captains Bill N4BTB and Don KQ4PCR. Please feel free to direct your questions to one of them or the Coach/Control Operator on duty.
